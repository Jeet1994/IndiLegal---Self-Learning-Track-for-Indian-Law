## üìö Curriculum Structure

### Month 1: Foundations of Law & Constitution
**Focus:** Building fundamental understanding
- Introduction to legal systems
- Indian Constitution basics
- Fundamental Rights & Duties
- Union and State governance

**Key Resources:** Constitution of India (Bare Act), M.P. Jain

### Month 2: Criminal Law - Part I
**Focus:** Understanding criminal liability
- IPC general principles
- Offenses against human body
- Offenses against property
- Offenses against women & children

**Key Resources:** PSA Pillai, Ratanlal & Dhirajlal

### Month 3: Criminal Procedure & Civil Law Basics
**Focus:** Procedural aspects
- CrPC - Investigation & arrest
- Bail and trial procedures
- CPC - Jurisdiction
- Pleadings and service

**Key Resources:** Mulla on CPC, CrPC Bare Act

### Month 4: Contract Law & Tort Law
**Focus:** Private law fundamentals
- Indian Contract Act
- Performance and breach
- Law of Torts
- Negligence and liability

**Key Resources:** Pollock & Mulla, Ratanlal on Torts

### Month 5: Family Law & Property Law
**Focus:** Personal and property rights
- Hindu Law
- Muslim & Christian Law
- Special Marriage Act
- Transfer of Property Act

**Key Resources:** Paras Diwan, R.K. Sinha

### Month 6: Special Laws & Revision
**Focus:** Contemporary legislation & consolidation
- Consumer Protection Act
- IT Act & Cyber Laws
- Labour Laws
- Comprehensive revision

**Key Resources:** Universal's guides, Recent judgments

## üèÜ Tips for Success

### Study Strategies

1. **Active Reading**
   - Don't just read - analyze
   - Question every principle
   - Connect concepts across subjects

2. **Case Law Method**
   - Facts ‚Üí Issues ‚Üí Arguments ‚Üí Judgment ‚Üí Ratio
   - Create one-page summaries
   - Focus on legal principles, not just outcomes

3. **Note-Making**
   - Subject-wise notebooks
   - Use flowcharts and diagrams
   - Highlight key sections of bare acts
   - Create comparison charts

4. **Regular Revision**
   - Daily: 30 minutes previous day's topics
   - Weekly: 2 hours entire week
   - Monthly: 4-6 hours entire month
   - End of 6 months: Comprehensive revision

5. **Practice Writing**
   - Weekly essay on legal topics
   - Draft sample documents
   - Write case commentaries
   - Practice legal reasoning

### Common Pitfalls to Avoid

‚ùå **Don't:**
- Skip bare act reading
- Rely only on short notes
- Memorize without understanding
- Study irregularly
- Ignore landmark judgments
- Neglect current developments

‚úÖ **Do:**
- Read primary sources first
- Understand concepts deeply
- Study consistently
- Analyze multiple cases
- Follow recent judgments
- Engage in discussions

### Staying Motivated

- Join online legal study groups
- Follow legal professionals on social media
- Attend free webinars
- Track your progress visually
- Celebrate monthly milestones
- Connect with fellow learners
