6-Month Learning Roadmap

Month 1: Foundations of Law & Constitution

Build your fundamental understanding of legal systems and constitutional framework

Week 1: Introduction to Law - Concepts, sources, and legal systems
Week 2: Indian Constitution - Preamble, fundamental rights, and features
Week 3: Directive Principles and Fundamental Duties
Week 4: Union and State governance, Parliament, and Legislature
Key Focus: Read the Constitution of India (Bare Act). Study landmark cases like Kesavananda Bharati v. State of Kerala.

Month 2: Criminal Law - Part I

Understanding criminal liability and offenses under IPC

Week 1: Introduction to IPC - General principles and exceptions
Week 2: Offenses against the human body - Murder, culpable homicide, hurt
Week 3: Offenses against property - Theft, robbery, extortion
Week 4: Offenses against women and children
Key Focus: Study IPC sections 300-304 (murder vs culpable homicide). Analyze cases like K.M. Nanavati v. State of Maharashtra.

Month 3: Criminal Procedure & Civil Law Basics

Learn procedural aspects and introduction to civil law

Week 1: CrPC - FIR, investigation, and arrest procedures
Week 2: Bail, trial procedures, and appeals
Week 3: Introduction to CPC - Jurisdiction and suits
Week 4: Pleadings, summons, and service of process
Key Focus: Understand Article 20, 21, 22 of Constitution. Study bail jurisprudence and procedural safeguards.

Month 4: Contract Law & Tort Law

Essential private law subjects for legal practice

Week 1: Indian Contract Act - Formation and validity
Week 2: Performance, breach, and remedies
Week 3: Law of Torts - Negligence, defamation, nuisance
Week 4: Strict and vicarious liability, remedies
Key Focus: Study Carlill v. Carbolic Smoke Ball Co., Donoghue v. Stevenson, and M.C. Mehta cases.

Month 5: Family Law & Property Law

Personal laws and property rights in India

Week 1: Hindu Law - Marriage, divorce, and adoption
Week 2: Hindu Succession and partition
Week 3: Muslim, Christian, and Special Marriage Act
Week 4: Transfer of Property Act - Sale, mortgage, lease, gift
Key Focus: Study 2005 amendment to Hindu Succession Act. Understand concept of coparcenary and property rights.

Month 6: Special Laws & Revision

Contemporary legislation and comprehensive revision

Week 1: Consumer Protection Act, RTI Act, and environmental laws
Week 2: IT Act, cyber laws, and data protection
Week 3: Labour laws and industrial disputes
Week 4: Comprehensive revision and case law analysis
Key Focus: Study recent Supreme Court judgments. Practice legal drafting and opinion writing.
